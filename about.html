Jenny Cho, L.Ac.

Jenny Cho is a California Acupuncture Board Licensed Acupuncturist (L.Ac.) and the owner of C.V. Wellness Center.

Jenny attended Five Branches University and acquired a Masters degree in Traditional Chinese Medicine to pursue her passion for Eastern medicine and natural healing. After spending several years assisting at acupuncture clinics in San Jose, she opened C.V. Wellness Center in May 2018.
